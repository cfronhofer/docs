---
title: 'How do I change my password?'
---
You can change your password at any time by going to the [Account Settings](https://readwise.io/preferences/account) page (accessible via the account menu in the upper right corner of any page) and then clicking [Change](http://readwise.io/accounts/password/reset/) next to the platform field.
